Raiden Shogun

The Raiden Shogun (Japanese: 雷らい電でん将しょう軍ぐん Raiden Shougun), real name Raiden Ei (Japanese: 雷らい電でん影えい Raiden Ei), is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact. She controls a puppet while meditating inside the Plane of Euthymia. Her current form is the vessel of Beelzebul, the current Electro Archon of Inazuma.
